## ATTORNEY REVIEW NOTES

1. **Residual Calculation Liability:** This is the #1 legal risk. If our platform miscalculates residuals, ISOs could underpay or overpay agents by thousands. The disclaimer language needs to be bulletproof. Consider a specific acknowledgment/click-through for financial calculation features.
2. **SSN Liability:** Clarify the liability chain — if an ISO uploads SSNs without proper consent, who's liable? The indemnification clause should cover this clearly.
3. **AI Liability:** AI-extracted SSNs or financial data that are wrong could cause real harm. Consider requiring explicit user verification before any AI-extracted data is committed to the system.
4. **GLBA Service Provider Agreement:** ISOs may need us to sign a GLBA-compliant service provider agreement. Consider whether to incorporate this into the ToS or keep it as a separate agreement.
5. **Arbitration vs. Litigation:** At early stage with smaller customers, arbitration may be more cost-effective. Revisit for enterprise contracts.
6. **Auto-Renewal Compliance:** Several states have auto-renewal disclosure requirements (CA, NY, IL, etc.). Ensure billing section complies.
7. **Data Portability:** Consider guaranteeing data export in a standard format (CSV) upon termination — this is increasingly expected and may become legally required.
8. **Subprocessor Changes:** Should customers be notified before we add new subprocessors? Standard practice is 30-day advance notice with right to object.
