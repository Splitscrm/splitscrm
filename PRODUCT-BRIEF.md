# ISO CRM — Product Brief

**The modern residuals and merchant management platform built for ISOs who are tired of overpaying for IRIS or drowning in spreadsheets.**

---

## The Problem

You get residual files from 3 different processors in 3 different formats. You spend 2 days in Excel matching MIDs to agents, running split calculations, and praying nothing is wrong. Or you pay $1,799/mo for IRIS CRM and still export to Excel because their reports don't do what you need.

**There's nothing in between.**

## The Solution

**ISO CRM gives you automated residual calculations, smart file imports, and agent management for $99-149/mo — not $1,799.**

Drop in any processor's CSV. Our AI maps the columns automatically. Your agent splits calculate in seconds. Your agents check their own residuals. You get your weekends back.

---

## 6 Core Features at Launch

### 1. AI-Powered Residual Import
Drop any processor's CSV or Excel file into ISO CRM. Our AI auto-detects and maps columns — no manual drag-and-drop, no breaking when Fiserv renames a field. Works with Fiserv, TSYS, Worldpay, PayArc, Paysafe, Priority, and any other processor file you throw at it. Upload once, and it remembers the format forever.

### 2. Universal Residual Dashboard
See all your processors in one view. Every merchant, every MID, normalized into a single format. Filter by processor, agent, MCC, volume range. Month-over-month comparison built in — no more exporting to Excel to figure out what changed.

### 3. Flexible Split Engine
Profit splits, revenue shares, BPS overrides, buy rate programs — all supported. Multi-level hierarchies (you → super agent → agent → sub-agent) with per-merchant overrides and effective dates. Define it once, and payouts calculate automatically every month.

### 4. Agent Self-Service Portal
Each agent logs in and sees their merchants, their volume, their residuals. Drill down from portfolio → processor → merchant. Month-over-month trends. Exportable. **Eliminates 90% of "why is my residual lower?" calls and texts.**

### 5. Anomaly Detection & Alerts
- "Joe's Pizza volume dropped 42% — attrition risk"
- "Fiserv increased BIN sponsorship by 2 BPS — costing you $340/mo"
- "14 merchants had $0 volume this month"
- "Agent Sarah's portfolio is down 18% MoM"

Catches the errors and trends you'd miss in a spreadsheet.

### 6. Lead & Merchant Pipeline
Track leads from first contact to signed application to boarded merchant. No more separate CRM for sales and a different tool for residuals. One system, one login, one view of every merchant relationship.
